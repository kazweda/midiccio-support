# MiDiccio

**Offline-first personal vocabulary learning app**

Available on iOS and Android. No account required, no ads. Manage your vocabulary on your device.

---

## Features

- **Vocabulary management**: Add, edit, and organize words, meanings, notes, and tags
- **Backup**: Export and import in JSON format
- **Cloud sync (iOS)**: Manual and auto-sync via iCloud
- **Cloud sync (Android)**: Manual and auto-sync via Google Drive
- **Offline-first**: No server required, no account needed

---

## Privacy

MiDiccio respects your privacy:

- No personal data collected
- All data stored locally on your device
- Cloud backup is optional (your own iCloud / Google Drive only)
- No in-app analytics or tracking
