# MiDiccio

## Build your own private dictionary

No account.  
No tracking.  
No ads.  
Just your words.

---

## Why MiDiccio?

Most vocabulary apps are built around services:
accounts, forced syncing, ads, and data collection.

MiDiccio is different.

It's built for people who want full control over their own words.

- Your data stays on your device
- No sign-up required
- Works fully offline

**You own your dictionary.**

---

## Who is this for?

MiDiccio is for:

- Language learners who want a simple, distraction-free tool
- People who care about privacy
- Users who prefer owning their data over relying on cloud services
- Anyone who has ever felt other apps are "too much"

---

## What you can do

Capture and organize your vocabulary in a way that fits your thinking.

- Quickly add words anytime
- Instantly find what you need
- Organize with flexible tags
- Review your words without friction

No complicated setup. No unnecessary features.

---

## Designed for simplicity

MiDiccio focuses on doing one thing well:

**helping you build your own vocabulary.**

- Clean interface
- Fast interaction
- No distractions

You spend time learning — not managing the app.

---

## Your data, your control

Your words are personal.

MiDiccio respects that.

- Stored locally on your device
- Optional backup when you choose (iCloud / Google Drive)
- No hidden data collection

Nothing is uploaded without your intent.

---

## Offline-first

No internet? No problem.

MiDiccio works wherever you are:

- On a plane
- While commuting
- In places with poor connectivity

Your learning doesn't depend on a network.
